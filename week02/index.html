About Me

I'm a software engineering student from the desert lands of Henderson, NV, a city in Babylon the Las Vegas valley. I'm a nerd for computers, math, music and languages.

I began learning to program when I was about 10 years old and tried my hand at C, C++ and BASIC. C and C++ were a bit too advanced for me at the time, so I stuck to BASIC but dropped off from coding at around high school. I picked it back up a few years ago learning Java and JavaScript and have loved programming ever since.

Math is near and dear to my heart. I love seeing patterns in nature and knowing how to take advantage of natural forces throught the universe. I tutored calculus, differential equations and linear algebra for a few semesters on BYUI campus.

I play the piano and write my own music. I have perfect pitch and can pick out notes from virtually any sound in nature.

I took German classes in high school and can read most of the language. I served an LDS mission in Dallas, TX speaking Spanish and am fluent in it; as a result, I can now read Portuguese and Italian with ease and am learning French with my wife. I plan to learn Japanese, Korean, Mandarin, Russian, Hebrew, Arabic, Greek, and many more!
